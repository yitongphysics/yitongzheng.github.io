Yitong is a Ph.D. student in the Department of Mechanical Engineering and Materials Science and Integrated Graduate Program in Physics and Engineering Biology at Yale and joined Prof. Corey O'Hern's group in the fall of 2021. He received his B.S. degree in physics from Wuhan University, where he performed computational studies of polyelectrolytes with Prof. Zhi-Jie Tan. In the fall of 2019, Yitong was an exchange student at Duke University, where he carried out computational studies of polymer dynamics with Prof. Michael Rubinstein. His current research project focuses on how tissue mechanics affects the dynamics of tumor cell invasion.
